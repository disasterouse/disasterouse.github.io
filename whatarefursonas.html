"What is a fursona?", you might ask yourself.

A fursona is the representation of a furry. Simply put, it's like an alter ego, or a persona of someone. Fursonas are usually seen online, in art, or in the real world, be it whilst wearing a full fursuit, a partial suit, or just as a tail that you can buy, or just as someone calling themselves by their fursona name.
